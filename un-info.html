UN Goals Alignment

Elevate Youth Foundation is proud to align our mission with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals for a better tomorrow.

📖

SDG 4 — Quality Education

We strive to ensure inclusive and equitable quality education and promote lifelong learning opportunities for children in underprivileged communities via our tutoring, digital literacy, and scholarship programmes.

🍽️

SDG 2 — Zero Hunger

Through our community food drives and nutrition workshops, we are committed to ending hunger and ensuring access to healthy, nutritious food for all youth — because learning cannot happen on an empty stomach.

🧠

SDG 3 — Good Health & Well-being

Our mental wellness camps and health education programmes promote physical and emotional well-being among children and adolescents from marginalised backgrounds.

♀️

SDG 5 — Gender Equality

We actively ensure equal access to education and mentorship for girls, and run dedicated girl-empowerment workshops to break down systemic gender barriers.

🤝

SDG 17 — Partnerships for the Goals

We actively collaborate with global NGOs, government bodies, corporate partners, and academic institutions like Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham to scale our impact and effectively deliver life-changing initiatives.

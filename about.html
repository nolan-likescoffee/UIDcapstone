Our Mission & Story

Rooted in compassion, driven by purpose — discover who we are and why we do what we do.

✨ Est. 2018 — Serving 12+ Cities

Empowering Children,
Transforming Communities

Founded on the principles of empathy, resilience, and actionable hope, Elevate Youth Foundation is dedicated to breaking the cycle of poverty through comprehensive educational support and community empowerment.

We believe that every child from economically backward sections of society holds immense potential. By providing schooling, nourishment, and mentorship, we help them tap into their inner strength and create a brighter future.

🌸 Our Spiritual Inspiration

Mata Amritanandamayi
"Amma" — The Hugging Saint

Mata Amritanandamayi (Amma) serves as the guiding light and spiritual inspiration for our foundation. Her tireless dedication to humanitarian service — having personally embraced and comforted over 37 million people worldwide — shapes our every action.

"Where there is true love, anything is effortless."

Through her teachings on compassion and selfless service (seva), we strive to uplift the most vulnerable youth in our society with the same boundless love she embodies.
